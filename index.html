Spongebob SquarePants

Daily Activities

Spongebob can be found in the fictional town of Bikini Bottom where he works at the Krusty Krab as a fry cook. Many of Spongebob's hobbies include karate, jellyfishing, and bubble blowing. Spongebob has many friends that he enjoys doing his hobbies with such as Patrick and Sandy.


Design inspired by CodeAcademy

Jellyishing

Be careful not to be stung

Nothing is better to do on a sunny day then to go jellyfishing in the fields of bikini bottom. The jellyfish are caught and released and are only used to get a little bit of jelly to eat from them.

Karate

Practices with Sandy

Practicing karate with Sandy is a very enjoyable hobby for Spongebob and keeps him busy. Always on the lookout for the next surprise attack.

Bubble Blowing

Bubble Blowing Technique

The art of bubble blowing is a hard skill to master. It takes a lot of experience and practice. Not for everyone, but Spongebob and Patrick have it mastered.
